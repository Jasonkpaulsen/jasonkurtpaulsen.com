Jason Kurt Paulsen

Director of Technology · AI & Digital Transformation · North America

Jason Kurt Paulsen is a technology leader focused on driving AI-enabled transformation and enterprise platform strategy across North America. As Director of Technology at Estée Lauder Companies, he leads initiatives spanning omnichannel commerce, identity and access, enterprise data and analytics, and AI-driven capabilities—supporting tens of thousands of users and large-scale operational environments.

His career began unusually early, stepping into leadership roles in his early twenties where he managed large-scale customer operations and built support organizations from the ground up. By age 23, he was responsible for a 180-person operation, shaping both the structure and performance of the business. During this time, he also gained early exposure to emerging technologies such as virtualization, helping to form a foundation in how systems scale and evolve.

Over time, Jason developed a hybrid career across infrastructure, product, and business leadership—working across enterprise organizations, high-growth environments, and startups. He has led digital commerce transformations, built training and operational platforms for global retail organizations, and partnered with brands and leadership teams to modernize how technology supports the business.

He is particularly focused on the practical adoption of AI within large organizations. His perspective is that AI is not primarily a technology challenge, but an operating model shift—requiring new ways of working, decision-making, and ownership across teams. He is actively involved in advancing AI capabilities within the enterprise, with an emphasis on real-world application and measurable impact.
